QURE

February 2026 to Present

Made on Visual Studio Code
QURE is a learning-focused platform designed to understand and compare two major cryptographic approaches: classical RSA and post-quantum cryptography (PQA). The project highlights the limitations of RSA in the presence of quantum computing attacks and explains how post-quantum algorithms are being developed to address these vulnerabilities. It includes a demo chat interface with two modes, allowing users to conceptually observe how communication differs under RSA and post-quantum encryption. The primary focus is conceptual clarity—understanding why quantum threats matter and how cryptographic techniques are evolving in response.
